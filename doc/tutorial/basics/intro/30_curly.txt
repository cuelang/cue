-- text.md --
The outer curly braces may be omitted for the top-level struct.
CUE also allows both, which has a specific meaning.
[We will come back to that later](emit.md).

-- curly.cue --
one: 1
two: 2

"two-and-a-half": 2.5

-- expect-stdout-cue --
{
    "one": 1,
    "two": 2,
    "two-and-a-half": 2.5
}
